# Privacy Policy for Fine Zoom

_Last updated: June 30, 2026_

Fine Zoom does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or
user data.

## What the extension does

Fine Zoom adjusts the zoom level of the browser tab you are currently
viewing. It does this entirely on your device using Chrome's built-in
zoom API (`chrome.tabs.setZoom` / `chrome.tabs.getZoom`).

## Data collection

- **No personal data is collected.** The extension does not gather your
  name, email, location, browsing history, or any other personal
  information.
- **No data is transmitted.** The extension makes no network requests.
  Nothing leaves your device.
- **No tracking or analytics.** There are no trackers, cookies, or
  analytics of any kind.
- **No page content is read.** The extension never injects scripts into
  web pages and never reads or modifies their content.

## Local settings

The only value the extension stores is your preferred zoom step size
(1%, 2%, 5%, or 10%). This is saved locally in your browser for your own
convenience and is never sent anywhere.

Per-site zoom levels are remembered by Chrome itself, using its standard
zoom behavior — not by this extension.

## Permissions

Fine Zoom requests a single permission, `activeTab`, which grants
temporary access only to the tab you explicitly act on (by clicking the
toolbar icon or pressing a keyboard shortcut). It is used solely to read
and set that tab's zoom level.
